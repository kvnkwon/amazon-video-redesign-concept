Amazon Video Redesign Concept

    by Kevin Kwon

    Goal

    Bring Amazon Video's UI/UX into a more functional, modern, and enjoyable state while making it competitive with other similar services.

    Problems.

    Confusing and messy UI

    It is annoying to discern what you can or cannot watch with your membership. The prime videos should have its own page seperate from the ones that are for rent/buy. The varying sizes for different content (shows, movies, channels, and genres) contribute to the messy look.

    Does not feel like a video service app

    Amazon's video service just feels like an extended part of the store when it really should be its own app like its competitors. Amazon has a great amount of content to offer which deserves its own home.

    Looks dated compared to competition

    UI looks dated and falling behind other competitors. Show/movie content pages could be condensed and delivered in a cleaner format.
